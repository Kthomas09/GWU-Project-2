When analyzing the committee appointments in the US Senate for 2018, it is inconclusive as to whether there is a correlation between the committee appointment and campaign contributions to the individual's campaign. There would need to be more investigation into ranking the committiees, along with the amount the Senator raises for the political party on the whole. An important aspect to note is the difficulty to quantify the importance of the committees, as power of a committee is multifaceted and cannot be narrowed to "funding dollars".









Bubble Chart

This bubble chart analysis is to look at the amount of money collected for the individual's campaign and the total number of votes. This sampling does illistrate that Diane Finstine has the largest amount of votes. However, it is important to note that she is from a largely populated state as well. Thus it cost more money to get the candidate's message to more people in California. There must be further investigation to analyze this effect.
